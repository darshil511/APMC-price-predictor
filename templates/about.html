About APMC Price Predictor

The APMC Price Predictor is designed to help farmers and traders predict the future prices of agricultural commodities. Using Machine Learning models like ARIMA, the platform provides accurate price trends based on historical data.

✨ Key Features:

  • 📊 Real-time price trend analysis for crops.
  • 🔔 Notifications for registered crops via SMS/WhatsApp.
  • 📈 Interactive dashboard with dynamic chart filtering.
  • 🔍 Predictive analytics using uni-variate ML models.
  • 📌 User-friendly interface with mobile responsiveness.

🚀 Our Mission:

To empower farmers and traders with data-driven insights, ensuring better decision-making and optimized profits in the agricultural sector.

📖 User Guide

Step 1: Register & Login

New users must first sign up by providing their name, email, and phone number. If you already have an account, log in using your credentials.

Step 2: Register Crops for Price Tracking

Once logged in, navigate to the Profile Page and select your crops from the available categories (Vegetables, Fruits, Commodities). These registered crops will be used for price tracking and notifications.

Step 3: View Dashboard & Analyze Price Trends

Navigate to the Dashboard to view interactive charts for all your registered crops. Features of the Dashboard:

  • 📅 View past 7, 15, 30 days, or 6 months of price data.
  • 📈 Full-screen charts for better visualization.
  • 💡 Real-time updates on price fluctuations.

Step 4: Receive Notifications

If there are significant price changes, you will receive notifications via SMS/WhatsApp for your registered crops. This helps you make informed decisions on selling or storing your crops.

Step 5: Predict Future Prices

Using the Predictor Tool, enter your desired crop and see the forecasted prices for the next few days. This enables better planning and financial security.

Step 6: Contact Us for Queries

If you have any questions or feedback, visit the Contact Page and send us a message.
